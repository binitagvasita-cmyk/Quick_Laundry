\u26a0\ufe0f Important Service Disclaimer

Please read carefully before using our services. We care about your garments and want you to be fully informed about potential risks.

Color Bleeding & Fading

Some fabrics may experience color changes during cleaning, especially if they are not colorfast or have not been pre-treated by the manufacturer. We cannot guarantee against color bleeding or fading during the wash or dry clean process.

Stain Removal

While we use professional-grade cleaning agents and advanced techniques, we cannot guarantee complete removal of all stains. Old, set-in stains or certain types of stains may be permanent and resistant to any cleaning method.

Dry Cleaning Results

The effectiveness of dry cleaning depends on fabric type, stain nature, and garment age. We cannot guarantee 100% restoration of all garments to their original condition. Some damages may be irreversible.

Fabric Damage & Wear

Pre-existing damage, weak fibers, or manufacturer defects may become apparent or worsen during the cleaning process. Delicate fabrics, embellishments, and old garments carry inherent risks during any cleaning method.

What We DO Guarantee

While we can't guarantee perfect results in all cases, here's what we promise to deliver:

  • Professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly cleaning agents
  • Experienced staff trained in fabric care and stain treatment
  • Thorough inspection of your garments before processing
  • Honest communication about potential risks for your items
  • Industry-standard best practices and techniques
  • Careful handling and attention to detail throughout the process
  • Quick turnaround time for most services
  • Fair pricing with no hidden charges

Ready to Use Our Services?

We recommend inspecting your garments and informing us of any special concerns or stains before service. Our team will provide honest advice about the best course of action.
